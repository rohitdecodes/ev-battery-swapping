What is EV Battery Swapping?

EV battery swapping is a method where electric vehicle batteries can be quickly exchanged at swap stations, instead of waiting to recharge. This saves time and makes EVs more convenient, especially for commercial or fleet vehicles.

How Does It Work?

  • Drive to a battery swap station.
  • Remove the discharged battery from your EV.
  • Swap it with a fully charged one from the station.
  • Drive away with minimal delay (usually under 5 minutes).
